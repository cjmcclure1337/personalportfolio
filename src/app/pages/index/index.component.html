Projects

Condor Financial

Summary

A web application for a fictional financial institution that tracks various investment opportunities such as stocks and foreign currency exchange (forex) with frequent updates and also other types of investments such as a menu of sample certificates of deposit (CDs). You can freely add money to your account and invest it in these options, watching how your return evolves in real-time.


Scope

This project was done by a team of 6 people over the course of about 3 weeks. The Forex Marketplace API to update and display currency exchange rates and the Portfolio API to maintain all of a specific user’s investments in various assets were both solo projects by me developed as Express servers in Node.js using postgres to manage our data in SQL databases. On the front end, I implemented the components tied to those APIs and the full Buy/Sell functionalities for all assets.
